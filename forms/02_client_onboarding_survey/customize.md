# Customizing `Client Onboarding Survey`

Deep customer research survey. Send this to every new client right after
purchase. The answers are gold for refining your offer + copy.

## What to swap before deploying

1. **Title** — change `Client Onboarding Survey` if you want it branded to
   your program (e.g. `Welcome — Tell Me About You`).
2. **References to "me" / "my content"** — the questions are written from
   coach to client (`What made you choose me?`). Leave as-is if you're the
   one running the program; reword if it's being sent from a brand rather
   than a person.
3. **Webhook URL** — edit `webhooks.json` to send responses to your own
   onboarding automation (Slack notification, Notion DB, etc).
